# LaunchLens

## Register
product

## Product purpose
LaunchLens is an AI market-intelligence co-pilot. A founder types a product idea in
plain English; the agent researches it live, fuses **demand** signals (Google Trends,
Shopping, News via SerpApi) with **supply** reality (Amazon search, product reviews,
pricing via Oxylabs), and returns a decisive **Go / No-Go / Niche** verdict covering
demand, price band, differentiation, and positioning. It is conversational, keeps the
full chat per session, and remembers prior verdicts across every session.

## Users
Solo e-commerce founders, Amazon/D2C sellers, and indie product makers deciding what
to launch next. They are analytical and time-pressed: they want a confident call plus
the evidence behind it, not a wall of dashboards. They evaluate several ideas a week and
switch markets (India, US, UK). They read in daylight, between other tasks.

## Brand & tone
A senior market analyst, not a hype bot. Calm, sharp, evidence-led, decisive. It gives
you the call and shows its work. Confident without exclamation marks. Precise language,
real numbers, zero filler. Trust is the whole product: a wrong-but-confident answer is
worse than a hedged one.

## Theme (scene)
A founder at a desk in daylight, scanning a launch decision between meetings, wanting a
calm, legible, confident read. This forces a **light** interface: bright, low-noise,
high-legibility, the verdict immediately readable.

## Color strategy
**Restrained.** Tinted near-white neutrals (toward an indigo-violet hue) + one indigo
accent used only for primary actions, the active chat, focus, and live state. The only
saturated color on the surface is **semantic**: green / red / amber on the verdict, blue
for demand, amber for supply. Color always means something; it is never decoration.

## Strategic principles
1. **The verdict is the hero.** Go/No-Go/Niche must be the first thing the eye lands on.
2. **Evidence on tap.** Demand and supply signals are visible (research rail, product
   cards), so the founder trusts the call.
3. **Conversation with memory.** Full turn-by-turn history per chat; durable launch
   verdicts recalled across every session.
4. **Disappear into the task.** Earned familiarity (Linear / Stripe / Notion calibre);
   no invented affordances, no gratuitous motion.

## Anti-references
- Generic AI chatbots and "hero-metric" SaaS dashboards.
- Dark "tools look cool dark" themes; neon-on-black crypto aesthetics.
- Category-reflex palettes (finance navy+gold, AI purple-glow gradients everywhere).
- Decorative gradients, glassmorphism, gradient text, em-dash copy.
